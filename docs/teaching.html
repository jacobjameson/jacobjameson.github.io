Teaching

I began teaching in a Philadelphia summer credit-recovery program after undergrad and later spent two years as a middle school math teacher in New Haven through Teach For America. I served as 8th Grade Chair, consulted on math curriculum development for Achievement First, and completed Code.org’s professional learning program for CS educators. I love teaching and enjoy making quantitative and computational ideas accessible to a wide range of students.
